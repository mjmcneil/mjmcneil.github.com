Welcome

Let me introduce myself, I'm a student at the University of Waterloo in Ontario, Canada. I'm studying Systems Design Engineering where I like to focus on how we as a society interact with technology linked with user experience.
Outside of the classroom I'm involved with engineering student life. I am currently the Vice President Education for the Engineering Society 'B' and I'm a two time orientation Aerial Photo director. To learn more about my extra curricular interest please view my interests page.
As a Co-op student I have the opportunity to experience many different industries including financial, non-profit, telecommunications, and product development. It is through these jobs where I have developed my skills as well as learn many more. To learn more about my work place experience please view my experience page.
I would love to get in touch with you via email or linkedin.
